Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you start

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Email us at hello@superbwellness.co.uk and we'll get back to you.

Is Superb GDPR compliant?+

Yes. Superb is built to UK GDPR standards. Anonymous check-ins and feedback are never linked to an individual employee, and all data is hosted within the EU. Full details are available in our Privacy Policy.

Do employees need to download an app?+

No download is required. Superb Employee works directly in a web browser on desktop or mobile, and can also be added to a phone's home screen for an app-like experience.

Can managers see who submitted anonymous feedback?+

No. When an employee chooses to submit feedback or a weekly check-in anonymously, no reference to their identity is stored anywhere in the system. It is technically impossible to trace it back to them.

What happens to our data if we cancel?+

Manager and employee account data is deleted within 30 days of account closure. You can request full deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

How much does Superb cost?+

Plans start from £149 per month for up to 25 employees, with a free 2 month trial and no card required to start. Pricing scales with company size, and optional add-ons such as holiday management and training courses can be added to any plan.

Is there a free trial?+

Yes. Every new account includes a free 2 month trial with no payment card required to sign up.
